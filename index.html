About this Movement

Sometime last May 2025, I received the message from the Lord, that He wants us to have a 24/7 prayer movement for the nation. In which every hour, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, there will be someone and/or some group from the body of Christ who will pray in tongues. Right now, as I write this post, I'm experiencing the strong presence of the Lord. 🙇‍♂️🔥 God has a great plan for our country, the Philippines, in this great revival.

I want to share something God showed me in prayer yesterday. I believe this is not just for me, but for all of us who are standing for our nation. While preparing for our prayer meeting, I saw a vision. It was the map of the Philippines—and above the islands, I saw many tongues hovering in the air. As I looked, I heard the Lord say, “I am looking for tongues to use—tongues that will pray for the things I want to do in your nation.” I believe these tongues represent people who are willing to pray, intercede, and declare God's Word over the Philippines.
